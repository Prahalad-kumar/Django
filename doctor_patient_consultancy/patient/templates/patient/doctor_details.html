About Dr. Kumar

Dr. Prahlad Kumar is a distinguished cardiologist with over 15 years of clinical experience in interventional cardiology. He specializes in managing complex cardiac conditions, including coronary artery disease and heart failure. Dr. Kumar is renowned for his patient-centric approach, ensuring that every treatment plan is tailored to the individual's needs.

Education & Certifications

  • Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology Cleveland Clinic, Ohio, USA (2010 - 2012)
  • M.D. (Doctor of Medicine) All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi (2004 - 2009)
  • MBBS Bangalore Medical College (1998 - 2004)

Work Experience

  • Senior Consultant Cardiologist Apollo Hospital, Bangalore (2015 - Present)
  • Consultant Cardiologist Fortis Hospital, Bangalore (2012 - 2015)
  • Resident Cardiologist AIIMS, New Delhi (2009 - 2012)
